# LICENSE - TBD

The official license for the **AFRION Sahara** public repository is currently under review.

The goal is to select a license that balances public ecosystem growth with the "open-core" boundary defined in the [README](README.md).

## Candidates

1. **Apache-2.0**: Permissive, includes a patent grant. Good for broad industry adoption.
2. **MIT**: Simplest permissive license.
3. **AGPL-3.0**: Strong copyleft. Ensures that improvements made by hosted service providers are shared back, protecting the pan-African mission from closed forks.

## Decision Mechanism

- Choice will be finalized by the AFRION-SAHARA core maintainers.
- Contributions made during this TBD period are governed by the [CLA](CLA.md).
