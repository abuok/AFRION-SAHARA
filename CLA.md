# AFRION Sahara — Contributor License Agreement (CLA) (Summary)

**Important:** This is a plain-language summary to communicate the intent of the CLA.
**TBD:** A final legal CLA text should be reviewed by counsel when feasible.

## 1) Why a CLA exists (locked intent)
AFRION Sahara is being built under a **“hype now, ownership later”** strategy.
We need a CLA so AFRION can:
- build and ship Sahara as a product
- use contributions commercially
- sublicense contributions to partners/customers
- relicense the project later if needed
- keep IP clean for future funding, partnerships, and distribution

## 2) What you grant to AFRION (required capabilities)
By submitting a contribution, you grant AFRION a license that is:
- worldwide
- perpetual
- irrevocable
- royalty-free
- transferable
- sublicensable

This license allows AFRION to:
- use, reproduce, modify, display, perform, and distribute your contribution
- create derivative works
- combine it with other software and distribute under different terms (relicensing)

## 3) You keep your copyright (Option B)
Under the CLA approach chosen for now:
- You typically **retain copyright** in your contribution
- You grant AFRION the broad rights above so AFRION can ship and scale

## 4) Your promises (important)
You represent that:
- you have the right to contribute the code/material
- your contribution does not violate any employment agreement
- your contribution does not include third-party proprietary material

## 5) No secrets, no private data
Do not contribute:
- confidential information
- secrets/keys
- private datasets
- non-public scraped content
- copyrighted content you don’t have rights to share
